How to play:

This is just like your regular game of Tic Tac Toe; but space themed! You, the player, can choose between two roles: Alien or Astronaut. Whichever role you don't choose, the computer will use to play against you. In the game, click on an empty space you want to claim with your role. The first player to fill three spaces with their role on either a horizontal, vertical, or angular axis wins the round. The player with the highest score after 10 rounds wins the game! ‚òÑÔ∏è
